Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Introduction

SEO involves optimizing web pages to rank higher in search engine results. It is crucial for increasing online visibility and driving organic traffic.

Core Components of SEO

  • On-Page SEO: Optimizing titles, meta descriptions, and content structure.
  • Off-Page SEO: Building backlinks and promoting content.
  • Technical SEO: Ensuring fast load times and mobile compatibility.

SEO Best Practices

Effective SEO involves using relevant keywords, optimizing content for user intent, and ensuring accessibility across devices.
