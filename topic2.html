Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Overview

What is SEO?

SEO is the practice of optimizing web content to improve visibility and ranking in search engine results.

Why is SEO Important?

  • Increased Traffic: Higher rankings bring more visitors.
  • Better UX: SEO prioritizes speed, accessibility, and usability.
  • Cost-Effective: Organic search is free compared to ads.

Types of SEO Strategies

1. On-Page SEO

  • Optimize titles, meta tags, and headers
  • Use relevant keywords naturally
  • Strong internal linking

2. Off-Page SEO

  • Build backlinks from trusted sources
  • Social media and guest blogging
  • Brand mentions and online reputation

3. Technical SEO

  • Improve page load speed
  • Mobile-first design
  • Structured data, XML sitemaps

SEO Best Practices

  1. Use descriptive, keyword-rich URLs
  2. Compress and tag images with alt attributes
  3. Make sure your site is mobile-friendly
  4. Publish valuable, original content
  5. Update content and fix broken links regularly
